Explainable AI Summary

How the AI reached this result

The uploaded dermoscopic image is resized and preprocessed before being passed into the fine tuned MobileNetV2 model. The model produces probability scores for seven HAM10000 lesion categories, and the highest probability is selected as the primary prediction. Grad CAM is then generated to highlight the image regions that had the strongest influence on the model decision.
